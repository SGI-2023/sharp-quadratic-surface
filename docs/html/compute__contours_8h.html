Given a quadratic spline surface and a projection frame, compute the rational functions defining both the contour segments and the surface boundaries;.

Both (quadratic) parametric domain contour functions and (higher order) surface contour functions are computed. The indices of the corresponding patches for each segment are also extracted and a flag for whether they are boundaries.

An initial collection of intersections of the boundary and the interior contours are also computed, which exist for any planar projection.

Parameters
[in]spline_surfacequadratic spline surface
[in]frame3x3 matrix defining the projection
[out]contour_domain_curve_segmentslocal parametric domain contour segments
[out]contour_segmentssurface contour segments
[out]contour_patch_indicesspline surface patch indices for the contour segments
[out]contour_is_boundarytrue iff the patch is a boundary contour
[out]intersection_datadata for the intersections of the contours and boundaries
[out]num_intersectionsnumber of intersections
